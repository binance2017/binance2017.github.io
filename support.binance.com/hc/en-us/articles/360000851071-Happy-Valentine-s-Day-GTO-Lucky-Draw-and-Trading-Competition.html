Happy Valentine’s Day! GTO Lucky Draw and Trading Competition

Binance
  • February 11, 2018 09:47
Follow

Competition Time: 2018/02/12 0:00 AM (UTC) to 2018/02/19 0:00 AM (UTC)

Reward 1：Lucky Draw

Trade GTO (Buys minus Sells) on Binance during the competition period to earn a place in the lucky draw.

1. Trade GTO to win 100x Reward + Prize Pool

GTO net purchases  (Buys minus Sells) over 1 ETH:

  • One Lucky Binancian will win 100 ETH equivalent of Gifto;
  • Binance will then draw another 100 Binancians to equally split a pool of 200,000 GTO (2,000 GTO each).

2. Trade GTO to win 30x Reward!  

GTO net purchases  (Buys minus Sells) over 5 ETH, one Lucky Binancian will win 150 ETH equivalent of Gifto.

Reward 2: Net Purchases Competition

To celebrate Valentines, we have committed a total of 750,000 GTO to reward our fans worldwide. The Top 20 Binancians with net purchases (buys minus sells) during the competition period will be rewarded.

1st Place will receive 300,000 GTO
2nd Place will receive 200,000 GTO
3rd Place will receive 100,000 GTO
4th-10th: equally split a pool of 75,000 GTO
11th-20th: equally split a pool of 75,000 GTO

Note:

  • Binance reserves the right to cancel or amend the Competition or Competition Rules at our sole discretion.
  • All trades that we deem to be “wash trades” will not count towards your trading volume for this Trading Competition.
  • Winners from Reward 2 will not be able to participate in the Reward 1. Additionally, users will not be able to win the Lucky Draw more than once.
  • The Lucky Draw will be completed via live stream. More details to come.
  • The exchange rate of ETH and GTO shall prevail at the end of the event.

Thanks for your support!

Binance Team
